Pasti Al Huda

In Jalan Segambut, Kuala Lumpur, there is an Islamic preschool called Pasti Al-Huda. Its main goal is to provide early childhood education that incorporates both traditional academic courses and Islamic teachings. In a secure and nurturing setting, the center seeks to foster children's cognitive, social, emotional, and spiritual growth. Children study language and math in addition to fundamental Islamic beliefs, prayers, and lessons from the Quran. The center is an excellent option for local families who wish to provide their kids a solid Islamic foundation at a young age because it is created to assist young children in developing their morals and character.

SK Makmur

In Segambut, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, there is a primary school called SK Segambut Makmur (Sekolah Kebangsaan Segambut Makmur). With its emphasis on both academic and extracurricular activities, it provides education to students from a variety of backgrounds. The school aims to give kids an excellent education by supporting their growth in areas like leadership, athletics, and the arts while making sure they have a strong foundation in language, science, and math. SK Segambut Makmur is well-known for its dedication to encouraging its students' development and achievement.

SMK Segambut Jaya

Students from different backgrounds can receive an education at SMK Sejaya, a secondary school in Malaysia. In addition to co-curricular activities like athletics, clubs, and leadership programs, the school offers a diverse curriculum that covers courses like science, math, languages, and social studies. In order to prepare pupils for future school or the workforce, SMK Sejaya places a strong emphasis on helping them improve both personally and academically. In order to assist students in realizing their full potential, the school seeks to establish a creative and encouraging learning environment.
